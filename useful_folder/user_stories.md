Summary of Documents
These documents present Milestone 1 and 2 of a Software Engineering project by Team 11 from IIT Madras, focusing on developing PulseTrack HRMS - a GenAI-powered Human Resource Management System.
Problem Statement
HR teams and employees struggle with outdated, manual systems that are inefficient and impersonal. The solution is a GenAI-powered HRMS to revolutionize workforce management by making processes smarter, faster, and more human-centric.
User Categories

Primary users: HR teams, employees
Secondary users: Line managers/Team leads
Tertiary users: Executives, Regulatory bodies, IT administrators


All User Stories (16 Total)
HR Manager User Stories (6)

Dashboard Monitoring - Access real-time dashboard showing department-wise attendance and employee headcount to monitor workforce availability and team distribution
Job Description Management - Efficiently create and manage job descriptions with structured approach to save time and maintain up-to-date role definitions
Job Posting Management - Manage all job postings in centralized portal to view, edit, or close listings efficiently
Employee Database - Maintain and update employee personal, job, and contact details in one central place
Resume Screening - Reduce manual effort in screening resumes to focus on shortlisting qualified candidates faster
Policy Access - Make company policies accessible 24/7 through employee self-service portal for quick answers without HR contact

Employee User Stories (4)

Performance Tracking - View performance metrics and feedback to understand areas of improvement
Payslip Access - Access and download monthly payslips to track earnings and deductions
Leave Notifications - Receive timely notifications for leave approvals/rejections to stay informed
Policy Queries - Quickly find answers to company policy questions without waiting for HR responses

Team Lead User Stories (4)

Skill Development - Enable team members to access relevant skill modules to develop needed skills and improve team performance
Goal Setting - Set clear and effective goals for employees to improve their performance
Team Performance Dashboard - View dashboard to monitor team performance, progress, and motivate team
Employee Reviews - Review team employees to provide continuous feedback and help them improve

Executive User Story (1)

Strategic Analytics - Access high-level HR analytics and workforce insights through customizable dashboards for strategic decisions on hiring, budgeting, and workforce planning

IT Administrator User Story (1)

System Administration - Manage user roles, permissions, and system integrations securely to maintain system integrity, ensure data privacy, and support smooth HRMS operation
